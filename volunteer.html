Join the Team

Join us in serving the Lord at King Makers Chapel, where every act of volunteerism is a profound expression of faith and devotion. As we embrace the call to serve, we find purpose in uplifting others and spreading the love of Christ through compassionate deeds. Your participation strengthens our community, fostering bonds of fellowship and unity among believers.

Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Ushering & Security.

Ensure a safe and welcoming environment at King Makers Chapel. Join us in providing peace of mind and reflecting God's love through vigilant care and hospitality.

Kids Church.

Nurture young hearts with the love of Christ in a fun and engaging environment. Share Bible stories, teach songs of praise, and guide children in their faith journey.

Music Team.

Lead worship through song and praise, uplifting the congregation in God's presence. Use your musical talents to create an inspiring atmosphere that connects hearts to God.

Prayer Team.

Dedicate time to uplifting our community through prayer. Serve as a beacon of hope, interceding for needs and bringing spiritual comfort and guidance.

Administrative Support.

Provide essential behind-the-scenes assistance that keeps King Makers Chapel running smoothly. Join our team in supporting mission and outreach efforts.

Production Design.

Create visual experiences that enhance worship and communication at King Makers Chapel. Join our team in using creativity and technical expertise to glorify God.

Technical & Stage Management.

Support impactful worship experiences at King Makers Chapel. Join us in ensuring seamless audio-visual setups and stage management that enhance our services and events.

Communications.

Spread God's love and share King Makers Chapel's activities through various media channels. Join our team in connecting our church with the community through effective communication.
