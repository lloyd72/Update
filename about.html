We Are King Makers

King Makers Chapel is more than just a place of worship; it's a community where faith, love, and service intersect to create a vibrant and supportive environment. Our church is committed to the teachings of Jesus Christ, and we strive to be a source of inspiration and hope for all who join us.

At King Makers Chapel, we believe in the power of community and the strength that comes from walking in faith together. Whether you are seeking spiritual growth, looking to connect with fellow believers, or simply curious about our faith, we welcome you with open arms. Join us as we journey together, uplift each other, and make a positive impact in the world.

Our Purpose.

At King Makers Chapel, our purpose is to glorify God through worship, fellowship, and service. We are committed to building a community where faith is nurtured, love is expressed, and lives are transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit. Our goal is to create an environment where individuals can grow spiritually, find support, and experience the unconditional love of God. We strive to be a place where everyone feels welcomed and valued, encouraging each person to discover and fulfill their God-given potential.

Our Mission.

Our mission at King Makers Chapel is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ, foster spiritual growth, and serve our community with compassion and dedication. We aim to be a beacon of hope, guiding individuals towards a deeper relationship with God and each other. By providing opportunities for worship, teaching, and community outreach, we seek to equip believers to live out their faith in everyday life. We are dedicated to making a positive impact in our local community and beyond, reflecting the love of Christ in all that we do.

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

We believe that our true identity is found in Christ. As followers of Jesus, we are called to live out our faith authentically, reflecting His love, grace, and truth in our daily lives. Our identity in Christ empowers us to be bold in our faith and compassionate in our actions.

Church Is Family

At King Makers Chapel, we embrace the concept of church as a family. We are a community of believers who support, encourage, and uplift one another. In this family, everyone is welcomed, valued, and loved. Together, we grow in faith, share in joy, and bear each other's burdens.

Kingdom Culture

We are dedicated to cultivating a Kingdom culture that mirrors the values of God's Kingdom. This means living out principles of righteousness, justice, peace, and love in all aspects of our lives. We strive to be a transformative presence in our community, demonstrating the power of God's Kingdom through our actions.

Spirit Led Life

We are committed to living a Spirit-led life, seeking guidance and empowerment from the Holy Spirit in all we do. By being attentive to the Spirit's leading, we aim to fulfill God's purposes for our lives and our church. This involves a continuous journey of growth, obedience, and reliance on the Holy Spirit's wisdom and power.
